About Rose

Rose is one of the beautiful flowers in the world. The rose plant is an ornamental plant. People often plant it in their house pots. Due to its beauty and aroma it is planted in homes. The different colours of roses are Red,Pink and White

About Lotus

Lotus is the national flower of India. It is very beautiful and fragrant in appearance. The color of lotus flower is pink and white. These two colours are mainly found in lotus flowers. However, some purple colored lotuses are also found.

About Lily

The lily flower is beautiful and aromatic in appearance. It is noted for its beauty, aroma and shape. Its flower petals have brownish, or pinkish lines on the outside.

About Tulip

Tulip is a flowering plant in spring. This beautiful flower is found all over the world. There is a complete lack of smell in this flower. The look of tulip in enchanting, pure and mixed red, golden and purple shades captivates the audience.
